SOTU-db help

Welcome to SOTU-db: State of the Union Database!

SOTU-db is a tool for measuring the sentiment (positive or negative context) of the texts of US President's State of the Union addresses, called "SOTUs" on this site.


SOTU-db currently does four things:

  • Text Search: enter a search term, find instances where it occurs in a sample of available SOTUs. Sentiment for each sentence, as measured by a variety of sentiment lexicons, is returned as well.
  • Sentiment by Year: select a year and chunk size from a sample of SOTUs. SOTU-db returns the sentiment per chunk of words (based on your chosen chunk size) and the cleaned text upon which the sentiment analysis was performed.
  • File viewer: View the clear (reading) text for a selection of SOTUs.
  • Extras: View supplementary works (writings, multimedia, games, etc) submitted by contributors.

Please see "Documentation" in the top navigation bar to learn more about the texts, sentiment, and more.

SOTU-db is in a limited alpha release, so be sure to read the warnings to know what to expect.
